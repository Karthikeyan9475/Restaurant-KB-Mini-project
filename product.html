Our Recipe

At Kaiyendi Bhavan, our signature recipe for parota is all about delivering authentic street food flavors with the perfect balance of taste and texture. We start by using the finest maida, kneading it into a soft, elastic dough. After letting it rest, we roll and layer the dough to create the classic flaky texture that parota lovers crave. Each parota is pan-fried to golden perfection, ensuring a crispy exterior and a soft, chewy inside. Served hot with our homemade salna or flavorful curries, our parota is a delicious, filling meal that keeps our customers coming back for more!
